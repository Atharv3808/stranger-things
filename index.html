About The Event

Organized under the esteemed Association of Computer Science and Engineering Students (ACSES), Technophilia 3.0 is an electrifying technical extravaganza designed exclusively for the visionary minds of SGU Campus.

This dynamic event serves as a crucible where intellect meets innovation, fostering an immersive learning environment that propels students toward technological mastery.

With a core mission to empower, elevate, and evolve, Technophilia 3.0 delves into an array of technical domains—ranging from programming languages and decryption challenges to AI-driven chatbot development and beyond.
